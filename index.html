About OptFOOD

We're a dedicated platform connecting food donors with NGOs to minimize food waste and combat hunger in our communities.

Community Impact

Connect with local NGOs and restaurants to redistribute surplus food to those in need, strengthening community bonds.

Easy Donation

Simple, transparent platform for individuals and businesses to donate food easily and track their impact.

Reduce Waste

Minimize food waste by creating an efficient bridge between food surplus and food scarcity, helping the environment.

How It Works

A simple, transparent process connecting food donors with those who need it most.

1

Register

NGOs and restaurants create their free accounts on our platform with simple verification.

2

List Surplus

Restaurants list their surplus food available for donation with details and pickup times.

3

Match & Collect

NGOs browse and request available food donations through our user-friendly interface.

4

Distribute

Food is collected and distributed to those in need, with impact tracking for all parties.
